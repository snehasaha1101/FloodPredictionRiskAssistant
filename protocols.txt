FLOOD SAFETY PROTOCOLS

1. LEVEL: LOW RISK
   - Standard Operating Procedure: Monitor local news channels.
   - Action: Keep drains clear of debris. Check emergency kit batteries.
   - Contact: Local council office (555-0100) is open 9 AM - 5 PM.

2. LEVEL: HIGH RISK
   - Standard Operating Procedure: IMMEDIATE EVACUATION REQUIRED.
   - Shelter Locations: The Community Hall on High St. and the North School Gym.
   - Resources: Sandbags are available at the Fire Station (Zone B).
   - Health Warning: Do not walk through floodwater. Do not drink tap water without boiling.
   - Emergency Contact: Call 112 or the 24/7 Flood Hotline at 555-9999.
